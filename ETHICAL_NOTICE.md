This application is a **deliberately vulnerable web application** designed exclusively for:

- **Cybersecurity education** in controlled environments
- **Penetration testing training** with proper authorization
- **CTF (Capture The Flag)** competitions
- **Security research** in isolated lab environments

---

## Legal Requirements

**You MUST have explicit written authorization before testing any system.**

Unauthorized testing of computer systems is illegal under:
- Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA) - United States
- Computer Misuse Act - United Kingdom
- Similar laws in most jurisdictions worldwide

---

## Prohibited Uses

DO NOT deploy this application on public-facing servers
DO NOT use techniques learned here on systems you don't own
DO NOT use this in production environments
DO NOT share access credentials with unauthorized parties

---

## Authorized Uses

- Local development machine (isolated)
- Private Docker network
- Classroom/lab environments with proper oversight
- Personal learning in isolated VMs

---

## Security Researcher Code of Conduct

1. Always obtain written authorization before testing
2. Stay within the defined scope of engagement
3. Do not access, modify, or exfiltrate real user data
4. Report vulnerabilities responsibly
5. Follow your organization's security policies
